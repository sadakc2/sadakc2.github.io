2018 PEER MENTOR

I took on the role of Peer Mentor for the 2018 fall semester. As a Peer Mentor, I had a class of 20 freshmen I tought once a week for an hour and fifteen minutes alongside one of my favorite professors at Winthrop. I went through a last-third semester class spring of 2018 in preparation as well as a one week training course the week before the new freshmen arrived on campus. My faculty partner and I went over lesson plans each week in order to prepare for the class. We prep topics ranging from how to study, to how to manage time, to learning their personality types. I got to meet one-on-one with each student throughout the semester to get to know them better which allowed me to form a personal relationship with each student. Being able to help guide them through their transition from high school to college was challenging and time-consuming but was, by far, one of the most rewarding experiences of my college career.
